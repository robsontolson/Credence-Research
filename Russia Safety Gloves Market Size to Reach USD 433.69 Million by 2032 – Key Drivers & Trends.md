<ol>
<li><strong> Rising Workplace Safety Awareness:</strong><br /> Across Russia, heightened awareness of workplace safety is driving increased adoption of protective equipment, particularly safety gloves. Regulatory bodies and employer initiatives emphasize hand injury prevention, making gloves a critical part of safety gear in industrial environments. High injury rates in construction and mining sectors are also pushing demand upward.</li>
<li><strong> Growth in Industrial Activities:</strong><br /> The surge in industrial activities, including manufacturing, automotive production, metallurgy, and construction, is a key growth driver. These sectors pose significant risks of mechanical, chemical, and thermal injuries, necessitating the use of specialized safety gloves. As industrial output rises, so does the need for protective handwear that complies with global and local safety standards.</li>
<li><strong> Technological Advancements in Materials:</strong><br /> Innovations in glove materials&mdash;such as cut-resistant fibers, thermoplastic rubber, nitrile coatings, and impact-absorbing fabrics&mdash;are enhancing the effectiveness and appeal of safety gloves. These advancements contribute to product differentiation and market expansion.</li>
